About Us

Welcome to Burger Shot! Established in 2005, we’ve been serving up delicious, mouth-watering burgers for nearly two decades. Our mission is simple: to create the best burger experience in town.

Our Story

Burger Shot started as a small family-owned burger joint in the heart of New York City. With just a few tables and a passion for quality ingredients, we quickly became a favorite spot for locals. Today, we’ve grown to over 50 locations nationwide, but our commitment to quality remains the same.

Our Burgers

At Burger Shot, we believe in doing things the right way. That means fresh, never frozen beef, locally sourced produce, and homemade sauces that add a unique twist to our classic recipes. Whether you’re in the mood for our signature "Big Shot" burger or something lighter like the "Mini Shot," there’s something for everyone on our menu.
